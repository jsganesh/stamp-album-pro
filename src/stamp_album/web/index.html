👋 Welcome to StampAlbum Pro
1 Design album pages
Drag shapes from the palette onto the canvas. Each stamp can be resized, moved, and styled freely — just like a desktop publishing app.
2 Edit properties
Select any element to change its position, size, border, fill, label, and font in the right-hand panel.
3 Add philatelic data
For stamps, fill in catalog number, denomination, perforation, and condition — all displayed below the stamp.
4 Export to PDF
When your page is ready, click Export in the toolbar to generate a print-ready PDF.
